WHAT WE DO

We manufacture multicolor Self Adhesive Labels or various substrates like paper, BOPP, PVC, PET, PE Or Metalized Paper. Our raw materials are of the highest quality. Together with our latest machines and dedicated panel of designers and visualizers we have varied choice & blend of techniques to make the finest labels. We supply labels to a multitude of markets like FMCG, Personal Care, Automotive, Pharmaceuticals, Cosmetics, agrochemicals etc.
